Play here:  http://finegamedesign.com/evolution/

Arrow keys:  Move.
Mouse:  Shoot.

Shoot all the zombies.
When you die, your player comes back and replays their actions.
        
A game made in 48 hours by 3 developers.

Ludum Jam 2012 August 24-26 theme is "Evolution".
